Questions

Things people ask us

Do I need to be home for pickup?
Nope! Just leave your bags in the agreed spot — lobby, door, or designated area. Our driver handles pickup and we'll notify you when it's been collected.
What's the minimum order?
Our minimum is 20 lbs, billed at $45.00. If your laundry is under 20 lbs, you'll still be charged the $45 flat rate. Final weight is confirmed at pickup — no surprises.
How does the $5 route day discount work?
Each building has a dedicated pickup day (Mon–Fri depending on your area). Book on that day and the $5 discount is automatically applied. You can still book on any other day — you just won't get the discount.
Can I request a specific detergent or folding preference?
Absolutely. Use the special instructions field when scheduling to tell us your preferences — fragrance-free detergent, fabric softener, cold water, specific folding styles, etc. We'll do our best to accommodate.
Will I get a confirmation?
Yes — as soon as you submit your booking, you'll receive a confirmation email with all your details. Our team is also notified instantly so we can plan your pickup route.
How many bags should I put out?
Give us your best estimate when booking (1, 2, 3 or 4+ bags). It doesn't need to be exact — the actual charge is based on final weight at pickup, not the number of bags.
